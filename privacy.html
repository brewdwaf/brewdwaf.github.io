Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

Paralele Istorice Publishing Dashboard stores only the information needed to operate connected creator publishing and analytics workflows.

Information processed

The service may process OAuth tokens, the authenticated TikTok account identifier, basic public profile information, selected video files, captions, publishing settings, publishing status, and basic post metrics exposed by platform APIs.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate the account, show the connected account and publishing settings, publish only explicitly approved videos, poll publishing status, and display post analytics for the creator.

Storage and access

Credentials are stored locally in a git-ignored credentials folder controlled by the Paralele Istorice operator. They are not sold, shared with advertisers, or used for third-party profiling.

Revocation and deletion

The operator can revoke platform access from the relevant platform developer console or account settings and delete locally stored credentials at any time.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Paralele Istorice account owner through the associated GitHub account.
