Paralele Istorice Publishing Dashboard is a creator publishing workflow used to prepare and publish original short-form history videos to connected social media accounts, including TikTok.

Use of the service

The service is used by authorized Paralele Istorice operators to upload original video content, review captions and publishing settings, and explicitly approve publishing actions.

TikTok integration

When a user connects a TikTok account, the service may request TikTok permissions needed to identify the connected account, review eligible publishing settings, publish approved videos, and display basic post analytics where available.

User responsibility

The operator is responsible for ensuring that uploaded content is original, accurate, and compliant with applicable platform policies and laws. The service does not publish silently; every publishing action requires explicit approval.
